Pac Bot Rules

  • Teams: 1-3 members
  • Max size: 30 cm x 30 cm x 30 cm (Length x Width x Height)
  • Max weight: 5 kg
  • Roles: One bot is Ghost, the other is Pac
  • Objective: Pac bot must reach destination without being caught by Ghost bot
  • Arena: Maze environment
  • Capture Rules: Ghost wins if it catches Pac; Pac wins if it reaches destination
  • Control: Ghost bot controlled by one player, Pac bot by another (opponents)
  • Registration: Online & on-spot available
  • Mandatory: Participants must bring their college ID card
  • Prize Note: If participants are less than 10, only 25% and 12.5% of the prize pool is awarded

Robo Rush Rules

  • Max size: 30 cm x 30 cm x 30 cm (Length x Width x Height)
  • Max weight: 5 kg
  • Max motor speed: 500 rpm
  • Task: Traverse the given path
  • Teams: 1-4 members
  • Event Format: One-time round, no trial allowed
  • Registration: Online & on-spot available
  • Mandatory: Participants must bring their college ID card
  • Prize Note: If participants are less than 10, only 25% and 12.5% of the prize pool is awarded

Robo Soccer Rules

  • Max size: 30 cm x 30 cm x 30 cm (Length x Width x Height)
  • Max weight: 5 kg
  • Control: Wired, wireless, or autonomous
  • Power Supply: Self-powered, onboard, max 12V DC (no external sources)
  • Only one participant controls the robot
  • No player exchange during match
  • Teams: Up to 3 members
  • Ball Handling: Push or hit only (no grabbing/holding), Ball size: 40 mm
  • Judges' decision is final
  • Arena setup by organizers on event day
  • Online & on-spot registration available

Robo Sumo Rules

  • Max size: 30 cm x 30 cm x 30 cm (Length x Width x Height)
  • Max weight: 5 kg
  • Teams: 1-3 members, one robot per team
  • Goal: Push opponent out of ring or immobilize them
  • Match duration: 3 minutes
  • Robots must stay in arena (circular or square with clear boundaries)
  • Control: Remote-controlled (wired or wireless)
  • Scoring: Win by staying in ring or pushing opponent out
  • If robot cannot move, opponent wins
  • Safety: No sharp parts or dangerous materials
  • Online & on-spot registration available
