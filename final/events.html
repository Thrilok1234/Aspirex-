OUR EVENTS

LINE FOLLOWER

Design and deploy your autonomous marvel to master the art of line tracking! Navigate twists, turns, and 90-degree challenges with precision on a black line over white or white line over black. It's a battle of speed and accuracy where the ultimate champ conquers the course in record time, staying glued to the line from start to victory!

ROBO SUMO

Robo Sumo is a competitive robotics sport where autonomous robots attempt to push each other out of a circular ring, mimicking the rules and strategies of traditional Japanese sumo wrestling. Design your robot to be powerful, stable, and strategic to become the ultimate sumo champion!

ROBO SOCCER

Enter the Arena of Futuristic Soccer supremacy! Command your remote-controlled/ wired or wireless robot, to dominate the specially designed robotic soccer battleground. Score big, strategize smart, and outmaneuver your opponents to clinch victory.
