ABOUT US

ARME

The Association of Robotics and Mechatronics Engineers (ARME), a student-driven force within the Society of Robotics and Mechatronics at AU-MIT, unites robotics and mechatronics engineering enthusiasts with a bold vision. Our mission is to ignite curiosity and steer young minds toward groundbreaking technologies and unconventional ideas. As a college association, we thrive on pushing boundaries, dreaming beyond the ordinary, and crafting a future where innovation knows no limits.

OUR WORKSHOPS

SENS-O-ROS - Conducted Smoothly

ROS + Sensor Integration Workshop

Join ARME at Madras Institute of Technology for an electrifying 3-day robotics workshop on April 4-6 at SRK Hall! Whether you're a beginner or a tech geek, this is your chance to learn, build, and innovate with hands-on projects and cutting-edge tech!

Workshop Schedule:

  • Day 1: Sensors, motors & coding—get hands-on from the start!
  • Day 2: Explore IoT with ESP32 & dive into ROS (Robot Operating System).
  • Day 3: Master ROS simulations & solve real-world robotics challenges!

Work with experts, build your own projects, and take home skills that set you apart!

Event Details:

  • Date: April 4-6
  • Venue: SRK Hall, MIT
  • Fee: ₹4000 per team (4 members)
